📚 18 Documents of PPAP

These are typically the AIAG standard 18 elements, listed below as in your source content.

Overview List
Sr. No. Document Name Description Detailed Description
1 Master Index Drawing or specification defining the part. Quick navigation list of all 18 PPAP documents
2 Design Record Records of approved engineering changes. Drawing No., Rev. Level, Part No., Description
3 Engineering Change Document Evidence of customer approval for design. Change request, reason, date, approval
4 Customer Engineering Approval Analysis of potential design failures and effects. Approval reference & date
5 DFMEA Detailed flow of manufacturing process steps. Function, Failure Mode, Cause, Effect, RPN
6 Process Flow Diagram Risk analysis of process failures and their effects. Step-wise process from receiving to dispatch
7 PFMEA List of control methods for process characteristics. Detailed process risk assessment
8 Control Plan Study verifying accuracy and precision of measuring system. Product & process controls with reaction plan
9 MSA Measured dimensions compared to design specs. Gauge name, Type, Study result
10 Dimensional Results Evidence of physical, chemical and performance testing. 10 sample results, tolerance, judgment
11 Material / Performance Test Results Process capability study for critical parameters. Lab report summary
12 Initial Process Studies (SPC) Certificates of accredited testing laboratories. Cp, Cpk charts summary
13 Qualified Laboratory Documentation Visual and aesthetic approval record. Lab name, NABL status
14 Appearance Approval Report (AAR) Actual parts manufactured under production conditions. Surface, Colour, Texture approval
15 Sample Production Parts Reference sample signed by customer and supplier. Sample lot, inspection, approval
16 Master Sample Gauges, fixtures, templates used for inspection. Stored sample info
17 Checking Aids Additional documents required by the customer. Gauge/fixture list
18 Customer Specific Requirements (CSR) Final summary document submitted to customer. Special requirements
19 Part Submission Warrant (PSW) Final sign-off summary
