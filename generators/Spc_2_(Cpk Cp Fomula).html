Input Rules & Validity Check

For meaningful Cp/Cpk:

  • USL must be greater than LSL.
  • Sigma (σ) must be positive (σ > 0).
  • Mean (μ) should be between LSL and USL for a capable process.

4. Interpretation – What Your Numbers Mean

Cp measures the potential of the process if it is perfectly centered. Cpk measures the actual performance considering both spread and centering. Once you calculate values, this box will give a quick text interpretation.

  • Cp < 1 or Cpk < 1 → process not capable.
  • Cp & Cpk ≈ 1 → just capable; no margin.
  • Cp, Cpk ≥ 1.33 → generally accepted in production.
  • Cpk ≥ 2.0 → world-class capability (very few defects).
